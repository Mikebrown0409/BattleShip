How to Play:

Objective: Sink all enemy ships before they sink yours!

  1. Placement: Select a ship, use the 'Rotate Ship' button to change orientation (Vertical/Horizontal), and click on your grid ('Your Ships') to place it. Place all your ships.
  2. Firing: Once placement is done, click on a square in the 'Enemy Waters' grid to fire a shot.
  3. Turns: Take turns firing at each other's grids. Red 'X' marks a hit, white ripple marks a miss. A sunk ship turns dark red.
